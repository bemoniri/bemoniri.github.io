Welcome to my home page.

My name is Behrad Moniri and I am an undergraduate student of Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology. I am interested in mathematical understanding of efficient algorithms for machine learning, signal processing, high dimensional statistics and causal inference. I am also very interested in the study of different phenomena in high dimensional probabilistic problems, e.g. concentration, universality and sharp transitions.

Experiences

Research Experiences

I am currently a member of the Signal Processing Research Lab at the Department of Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology. I work under the supervision of Prof. Massoud Babaie-Zadeh on Statistical Signal Processing and more specifically, on separability conditions for nonlinear mixtures of stochastic processes.

We have a High Dimensional Reading Group with Dr. Amin Gohari and Dr. Mohammad Hossein Yassaee, reading various papers and textbooks, focusing on High Dimensional Statistics, Sparsity and Theoretical Machine Learning.

I am also a very active member of CausalAI, a small journal club, led by Dr. Saber Salehkaleybar on Causal Inference.

Teaching Experiences
