Research

Currently, I am working on information-theoretic analysis of Bayesian learning. Some of our results have been submitted to ICML 2021. I am also interested in structured stochastic bandits. In particular, I use tools from high dimensionl probability to analyze the regret on non-parametric versions of stochastic bandits.
