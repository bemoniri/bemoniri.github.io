Research Experiences

Currently, I am working on information-theoretic analysis of Bayesian learning and the use of Rate/Distortion theory for analysis of Bayesian statistics.

We have a High Dimensional Reading Group with Dr. Amin Gohari and Dr. Mohammad Hossein Yassaee, reading various papers and textbooks, focusing on High Dimensional Statistics, Sparsity and Theoretical Machine Learning. I am currently active in a "sub-group" focusing on online learning, online convex optimization, and bandits. We are currently working on non-parametric versions of stochastic bandits.

From Summer 2019 to Fall 2020, I was a member of the Signal Processing Research Lab at the Department of Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology. I worked under the supervision of Prof. Massoud Babaie-Zadeh on Statistical Signal Processing and more specifically, on separability conditions for nonlinear mixtures of stochastic processes.
