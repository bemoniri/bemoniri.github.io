Welcome to my homepage.

My name is Behrad Moniri. I recieved my undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology with highest distinctions. I will start my Ph.D. studies at the University of Pennsylvania in Fall 2021.

Research

I am interested in the theory of machine learning, high dimensional probability, and information theory. Currently, I am working on information-theoretic analysis of Bayesian learning. I also work on regret analysis of non-parametric versions of stochastic bandits.
