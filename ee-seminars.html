Sharif EE Seminars

Welcome. This the webpage of Sharif EE Seminars, a weekley event organized by Resana Scientific and Cultural Association at the Department of Electrical Engineering, Sharif University of Technology. In these weekly talks, undergraduate and master students of the department, present topics of their interests.

The sessions are held at the virtual meeting room. For details about dates and time of each seminar, please refer to the posters. Also check out our Telegram Channel.

Questions? Comments? Want to present? Send me an email to Behrad Moniri: bemoniri@live.com
